BabyCenter apps take award-winning content on-the-go

BabyCenter asked Adamo to create the My Pregnancy Today and My Baby Today apps for iOS and Android.

The Work

BabyCenter asked Adamo Digital to create the My Pregnancy Today and My Baby Today apps for iOS and Android.

The Challenge

Reaching more than 40 million new and expecting parents with their award-winning digital content each month, BabyCenter is the number one parenting and pregnancy destination online. To continue meeting demands of an increasingly on-the-go audience, they needed to bring their popular pregnancy and baby-tracking website to mobile.

Working with a development partner that could act as an extension of their own team was essential. BabyCenter asked WillowTree to help them bring their mobile solutions to life.

The Results

Working with Adamo, BabyCenter launched two outstanding mobile solutions for new and expecting parents, My Pregnancy Today and My Baby Today.

The My Pregnancy Today and My Baby Today apps for iPhone and iPod Touch deeply integrate with BabyCenter’s internal database, delivering day-by-day pregnancy information, helpful videos, fetal images, checklists, nutritional guides, parenting articles, and more to users. As a result of their apps’ tremendously successful launch, BabyCenter retained Adamo to develop iPad and Android versions of their apps as well. When My Pregnancy Today for Android went live it took the space by storm, becoming one of the most quickly downloaded apps in the Google Play store at the time.

Today, BabyCenter’s apps see over 6 million monthly active users and 8 million monthly downloads. Beyond highly successful user retention rates, the BabyCenter apps have gone on to receive numerous consumer healthcare awards, and are now available to users all over the world.
