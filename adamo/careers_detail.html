Senior NodeJS Developer

apply for this job

Job Description

We have multiple teams working for our international clients, handling projects based on Javascript-style technologies, such as nodeJs, React Js, React Native and web technologies. As a developer, you will:

- Join a variety of projects for our international clients based on NodeJs platforms, to develop various aplications (web and mobile apps)

- Work as part of a team to meet demanding project requirements.

- Follow best practices and coding standards to ensure the best system performance regardless of the browser or device.

- Expected to design, code, analyze, configure, unit test, and maintain applications with minimal guidance

- Participate in Agile processes such as Scrum or Kanban.

Job Requirements

- A Bachelor’s degree in computer science or related field preferred

- At least 4 years real-world experience with software development.

- Proficient with Object Oriented Analysis and Design and its implementation into real code

- Very good in understanding & applying Design Patterns/Design Principles

- Proficient in code review, code refactoring

- Experience with a wide variety of front-end technologies a strong advantage

- Related certification is a plus

- Experience working with Web API, Web Services REST, SOAP, XML and related data exchange technologies is a plus

- Skills: NodeJs, React Js, React Native, Javascript/HTML5/CSS3, Database (i.e.: MySQL), SVN/Git, Knowing Jenkins/TeamCity is a plus, Agile process (Scrum/Kanban)

- Have the ability to work independently or as part of a team

- Must be a strong team player

Benefits and working environment

- Attractive AON healthcare coverage including health and accident insurance in addition to basic insurances regulated by the Labor Code. Annual company summer vacation and team-building budget.

- Potential opportunities for onsite work for international projects abroad.

- Organized training and a support in building your career.

- An active workplace with lots of out of work activities provided by the company and the Trade Union.

Submit application

To apply, please submit the following information:

- Job Application Letter with “NodeJs Developer” in the subject line.

- Updated CV in English, including your contact e-mail, phone number and salary expectations.
